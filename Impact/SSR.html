DS0017 Command Command Execution Monitor executed commands and arguments of binaries involved in shutting down or rebooting systems. For network devices, monitor executed commands in AAA logs, especially those run by unexpected or unauthorized users.
DS0013 Sensor Health Host Status Monitor for logging, messaging, and other artifacts highlighting the health of host sensors (ex: metrics, errors, and/or exceptions from logging applications) that may suggest the shutting down or rebooting of the system.
DS0009 Process Process Creation Monitor for newly executed processes of binaries involved in shutting down or rebooting systems.
